Personal Data (Privacy) Policy

At eBroker Limited (“eBroker”), we pledge to meet fully recognised standards of personal data privacy protection in complying with the requirements of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance. In doing so, we will ensure compliance by our employees to adhere to the strictest standards of security and confidentiality. We understand that the privacy and security of your personal data and account information are important to you. eBroker adheres to confidentiality standards that are designed to protect your personal information. When you browse the public (pre-login) areas of eBroker Website, none of your account and/or personal information is retrieved from our systems. When you choose to access the trading or portfolio areas, which are designed for account holders only, you are required to provide your eBroker login ID and your individually selected password, which you may change as often as you wish. From time to time, it may be necessary for Clients to supply eBroker with data in connection with the opening or continuation of accounts or provision of financial and/or other services. eBroker will also collect data on Clients in the ordinary course of business whilst your account is with us. eBroker shall at all times fully observe the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (the "Ordinance") of Hong Kong SAR in collecting, maintaining and using personal data of potential and existing Clients. The personal data provided by you on this website, from time to time, may be used for the following purposes: - The daily operation of the services provided to Clients; - Ensuring on-going credit worthiness of Clients; - Designing & marketing of our services and products to Clients; - Transferring and sharing of such data between eBroker and its affiliate companies; - Any purpose in connection with compliance with any law, rules, regulations, or order of governmental or regulatory authority. Data held by eBroker relating to a Client will be kept confidential but eBroker may provide such information to the following parties (whether within or outside Hong Kong): - Any agent, contractor or third party service provider who provides administrative, telecommunications, computer, payment, debt collection or securities clearing or other services to it in connection with the operation of its business; - Any other person or entity under a duty of confidentiality to it including its group company which has undertaken to keep such information confidential; - Any individual under a duty of confidentially, who is registered as the Client's Agent - Auditing or legal consultant commissioned by eBroker or the member of its Group - Any person to whom eBroker is under an obligation to make disclosure under any law, rules, regulations, directions, guidelines and/or codes applicable to eBroker. Under and in accordance with the terms of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, the Client has the right of access to and for correction of their personal data held by us. eBroker reserves the right to charge a reasonable fee for the processing of any data access request. When Client provides their personal information to eBroker through electronic means, eBroker generally uses best effort to maintain the security and integrity of its systems. However, due to many unpredictable traffic or other reasons, electronic communication may not be a reliable medium of communication. Clients should take attention of such weaknesses and communicate personal information through electronic devices with caution. This Policy may be revised or amended from time to time by eBroker. Client can access the most up-to-date version of this policy from our website.
